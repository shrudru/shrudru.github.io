  3. Hostel
    • Boys Hostel:

      • Malnad College of Engineering Boys Hostel started during the year 1961 cater to the needs of the students who are coming from far away places.
      Now, the hostel is very well equipped with modernized kitchen and huge dining hall which can accommodate nearly 260 students at a time for lunch/dinner.
      Hoysala, Hemavathi and Hasanambaare the 3 blocks which accommodate nearly 600 to 650 students in 324 rooms. All the rooms are provided with cots, tables,
      chairs and ceiling fans. Good and well maintained rooms, corridors, bath and Water closets.

      Generator facility, 24 hrs. Water supply, hot water for bathing and hygienic food, aquagard water for drinking is served to all the boarders of the hostel.
